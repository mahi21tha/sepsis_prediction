About qSOFA

qSOFA (quick SOFA) is a bedside screening tool for sepsis that can be performed quickly without laboratory values.

Criteria:

  • Respiratory rate ≥22/min
  • Altered mentation (GCS <15)
  • Systolic blood pressure ≤100 mmHg

Interpretation:

  • Score 0: Low risk
  • Score 1: Intermediate risk - monitor closely
  • Score ≥2: High risk - consider ICU evaluation
