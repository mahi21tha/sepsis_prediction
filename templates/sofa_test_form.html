About SOFA

SOFA (Sequential Organ Failure Assessment) is a comprehensive scoring system that evaluates organ dysfunction in six systems.

Organ Systems:

  • Respiratory: PaO₂/FiO₂ ratio
  • Coagulation: Platelet count
  • Liver: Bilirubin level
  • Cardiovascular: MAP and vasopressor requirements
  • CNS: Glasgow Coma Scale
  • Renal: Creatinine or urine output

Sepsis-3 Criteria: Acute increase in SOFA score ≥2 points in the presence of suspected infection indicates sepsis.
