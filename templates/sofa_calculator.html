About SOFA

SOFA (Sequential Organ Failure Assessment) is a comprehensive scoring system that evaluates organ dysfunction across six systems.

Sepsis-3 Definition: Life-threatening organ dysfunction caused by a dysregulated host response to infection, identified by an acute increase in SOFA score ≥2 points.

Scoring: Each organ system is scored 0-4 points, with higher scores indicating worse dysfunction. Total possible score: 0-24.
